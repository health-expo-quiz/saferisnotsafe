Vaping can cause fatal harm to your lungs. The chemicals in vapes may cause the irreversible popcorn lung, a scarring condition that is deadly.

Nicotine in vapes is highly addictive, especially for teens. 1 in 5 Aussie teens have tried vaping, and 10% are hooked!

Flavoured vapes might seem fun, but they often hide harmful toxins like formaldehyde, which has been proven to be carcinogenic (cancer causing).

Secondhand vape smoke can affect your friends and family, exposing them to nicotine and other nasty chemicals.

Disposable vapes are terrible for the environment, as millions end up in landfills with metal casings and plastic as well as leaking toxic chemicals into the soil.
